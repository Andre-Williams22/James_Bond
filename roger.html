Pierce Brosnan



Biography

Pierce Brosnan started his Bond career in 1994 after the retirement of his predecessor, Timothy Dalton. Pierce Brosnan's first film as Bond was GoldenEye in 1995. Brosnan starred in four films as the clandestine MI6 agent and hoped to do a fifth, but his contract was not renewed due to his high salary demands.
