      Ian Fleming


Ian Lancaster Fleming is known as one of the most famous and creative authors of his time.
He was the original creator of the majestic character. Ian Fleming was born on May 28,1908
in London, England and died on August 12, 1964. Fleming was born into a family of wealth due
to his grandfather being a Scottish banker. Fleming had the privilege of being educated in
England, Germany and Switzerland. His career started with journaling and later lead to
the clandestine lifestyle of high ranking officer in British naval intelligence during
World War II. After the war fleming fled to Jamacia where he began doing what we all
know him for, writing. The first Bond novel he wrote was Casino Royal(1953). This was
the first of fourteen James Bond novels. The books became popular in the United States
after president John F. Kennedy claimed that one of the twelve novels was his favorite.
