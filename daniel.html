Daniel Craig





Biography

James Wroughton Craig is an English actor. In his early years before his
career he attended the Guildhall School of Music and Drama in 1991. Craig
attained fame after his entrance into the prestige role of Ian Fleming's
British secret agent character James Bond. Craig took over for Pierce
Brosnan in 2005 and is still currently fulfilling his duty as the
charismatic and charming 007.
